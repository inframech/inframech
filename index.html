Finite Element Analysis (FEA)

Our company provides Finite Element Analysis (FEA) simulation services to help our clients optimize their product designs. FEA is a powerful tool that allows us to simulate the behavior of complex structures and systems under various conditions.

Our team of experts has years of experience in using industry-leading physics-based FEA simulation software to analyze product behavior, predict failures and creep, and optimize the product design accordingly. We offer a complete range of FEA consulting services, including;
  • Structural
  • Thermal
  • Fatigue
  • Stress Analysis
  • Dynamic
  • Flow

Structural design of the Two Unit Apartment Project - Eheliyagoda

Nestled within the city limits of Eheliyagoda lies a noteworthy project - a two-unit, three-story apartment complex. A prominent challenge our structural designers encountered was the presence of weak soil in the area.

Undeterred, our design team skillfully devised innovative foundation designs that not only addressed the soil challenge but also effectively minimized the need for costly traditional foundation solutions. This ingenuity resulted in an impressive 20% reduction in the substructure costs, significantly surpassing the initial estimation. Such accomplishments underscore our commitment to delivering cost-effective solutions without compromising on quality or integrity.
